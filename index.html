    About Us

    Mio Pranzo is the premiere online delivery service for ordering from Nairobi’s best restaurants and grocery stores. Browse through our diverse range of menus and when you order online from your favourite places they are always saved for easy and faster reordering, meal-planning and loyalty points.

    Mio Pranzo launched in 2022 with the aim of making it simple and easy for anyone to order food straight to their door. We now work with over 100 restaurants and over 5,000 customers in Nairobi and plans are underway to create new partners in other cities in Kenya and East Africa.
